About TastyExpress

From food delivery to your daily convenience companion

A subsidiary of Delivery Innovations Group, TastyExpress launched in Singapore in 2016 as a food delivery platform. Dedicated to helping customers get their tasty favourites fast, it quickly won the hearts and minds of customers across Asia.

Powered by tech and operational excellence, TastyExpress has been growing its quick-commerce footprint in more than 500 cities across 15 markets in Asia – including Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea, India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Cambodia, and Myanmar.

Thanks to dedicated partners, riders, and a team united by shared values, TastyExpress is now providing millions with a convenient way to get food and groceries in a few taps.
